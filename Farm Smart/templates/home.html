Banana

Bananas are tropical fruits that are widely consumed fresh or used in cooking. They are a major cash crop in many tropical regions.

Barley

Barley is a versatile grain used for animal feed, brewing beer, and as a health food. It is one of the oldest cultivated grains.

Chickpeas

Chickpeas are a legume crop rich in protein and commonly used in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisines, such as in hummus.

corn

Maize is one of the most widely grown crops globally and is used for food, fodder, and biofuel. It’s commonly used in processed foods and animal feed.

Cotton

Cotton is a major fiber crop used in the textile industry to produce clothing and fabrics. Cotton plants produce fluffy bolls that are harvested and spun into thread.

Millets

Millets are a group of small-seeded grasses that are hardy and drought-tolerant, commonly grown in dry areas for food and fodder

Oats

Oats are used as a cereal grain and are often consumed as oatmeal or used in baking. They are also grown as livestock feed.

Peanuts

Peanuts are a legume crop that is primarily used for oil production, animal feed, and as a snack. The seeds develop underground, unlike most other crops.

rice

Rice is a staple food for over half of the world's population. It grows in warm, waterlogged conditions and is commonly cultivated in flooded fields known as paddies.

Sorghum

Sorghum is a drought-resistant cereal grain that is grown for food, fodder, and ethanol production. It is often cultivated in arid regions.

Soybeans

Sugarcane is a tropical grass that is primarily used to produce sugar and ethanol. It is one of the most valuable crops in terms of revenue.

Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a tropical grass that is primarily used to produce sugar and ethanol. It is one of the most valuable crops in terms of revenue.

Tomatoes

Tomatoes are a fruit crop widely used in cooking. They are grown for both fresh consumption and processed into sauces, soups, and juices.

wheat

Wheat is a staple cereal grain that is grown in temperate regions around the world. It is used to make flour for bread, pasta, and other products.

Potatoes

Potatoes are tuber crops and are one of the most important food crops worldwide, used in various culinary dishes.
